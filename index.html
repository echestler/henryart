Hey, it's Henry. I'm an artist from Little Rock, Arkansas based in Moab, Utah.
My favorite part about making art is sharing it with you. Thanks for being here!
Enjoy this virtual gallery of some of my work. I have signed prints, postcards, and stickers available for sale. You'll find more info under each image.
I'm open to special requests, and I do commissions. Ask me about sliding scale pricing. Send questions, comments, and order requests to my email at henrybarker@gmail.com.
Check out my Instagram @henrybark_r for more.
